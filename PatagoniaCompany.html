About Us

Patagonia

Yvon Chouinard

About the founder: Yvon Chouinard was born on November 9, 1938, in Lewiston, Maine, United States. He grew up in a French-Canadian family and developed a love for the outdoors at a young age, spending much of his time climbing, fishing, and exploring the natural world.

Background: Patagonia was founded in 1973 by Yvon Chouinard as a small company producing climbing gear. Over the years, it evolved into a globally recognized outdoor clothing and gear manufacturer.

Goal: Patagonia's overarching goal is to create the best products without causing unnecessary harm to the environment. They aim to inspire and implement solutions to the environmental crisis.

Achievements:

Patagonia has been a trailblazer in environmental activism within the corporate world. They were one of the first companies to implement environmentally friendly practices, such as using recycled materials in their products and reducing their carbon footprint.
