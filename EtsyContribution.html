Etsy, Inc.

Robert Kalin

Etsy, Inc's Contributions to the Environment

  • Supporting Sustainable and Handmade Goods: Etsy provides a platform for independent artisans, makers, and small businesses to sell their handmade, vintage, and unique goods. By promoting products that are made with sustainable materials and production methods, Etsy encourages eco-friendly consumption and supports environmentally conscious sellers.

  • Reducing Carbon Emissions: Etsy is committed to reducing its carbon footprint and offsetting the environmental impact of its operations. The company invests in renewable energy sources, such as wind and solar power, to power its data centers and offices. Additionally, Etsy purchases carbon offsets to compensate for emissions generated by shipping and other activities, helping to mitigate its environmental impact.

  • Advocating for Environmental Policies: Etsy actively advocates for policies and initiatives that promote environmental sustainability and address climate change. The company engages with policymakers, industry partners, and non-profit organizations to support renewable energy adoption, waste reduction, and sustainable practices across the e-commerce sector.

  • Encouraging Eco-Friendly Packaging: Etsy promotes eco-friendly packaging practices among sellers to minimize waste and environmental impact. Sellers are encouraged to use recycled and recyclable materials, biodegradable packaging, and minimal packaging to reduce their environmental footprint. Etsy provides resources and guidance to help sellers make sustainable packaging choices.

  • Educating Sellers and Buyers: Etsy educates sellers and buyers about the importance of sustainability and eco-conscious consumption. The company provides resources, tips, and best practices on topics such as sustainable sourcing, ethical production, and eco-friendly living to help sellers and buyers make informed decisions that support environmental conservation.
